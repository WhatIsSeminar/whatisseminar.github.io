Credits

Obtaining credits for attending the $``\vec{w}h\alpha\mathfrak{t}\;\; i\mathbb{S}\ldots\text{?''}$ Seminar is only possible for BMS students enrolled either at the Freie Universität Berlin or at the Technische Universität Berlin, after successfully passing the BMS Qualifying Exam. The BMS Office decides if the student gets the credits (upon their regular attendance) and helps them with the credit transfer. If you are interested in getting credits for this event series or if you need more information about this, contact the BMS Office for more details.
